More About the Chef




Chef Farid first fell in love with cooking when he was a young boy in Morocco. He would often spend time in the kitchen with his mother helping her prepare food for the family. The love that mom would put in her cooking is the secret ingredient of Chef Farid's dishes today.

When he was a teen, Farid moved to Florence, Italy. He learned to prepare Tuscan cuisine along side the best Florentine chefs and in 2010 he opened his own restaurant, where he was able to blend his Moroccan roots with Italian classics.

Today Chef Farid enjoys bringing together friends and families at the table while sharing his unique and delicious culinary style.
